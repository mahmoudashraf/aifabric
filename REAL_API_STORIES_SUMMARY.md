## 🎨 Design Patterns Used

### Color Coding
- **Accent (Green):** Success states, positive outcomes
- **Destructive (Red):** Problems, negative states, alerts
- **Primary (Blue):** CTAs, highlights, important info
- **Secondary (Purple):** Additional information, medium priority

### Card Types
- **Product Cards:** E-commerce products with match indicators
- **Document Cards:** Legal documents with metadata
- **Transaction Flow:** Financial transaction visualization
- **Risk Indicators:** Color-coded risk levels
- **Comparison Cards:** Before/After scenarios
- **Impact Cards:** Business metrics transformation

- **Collapsible Story Acts:** Click to expand/collapse
- **Hover Effects:** Cards, links, buttons
- **Smooth Animations:** Scroll-triggered reveals
